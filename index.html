Meet Rhys, sailor turned techie.

With ten incredible years in the Royal New Zealand Navy, Rhys ascended to the esteemed rank of Petty Officer as a Propulsion Engineer. His skills were recognized, he topped his leadership courses while also being awarded the distinction of Sailor of the Quarter. The capstone of his naval career? Serving as an instructor for the Marine Engineering School, mentoring the next wave of talent.

Eager to explore new horizons, Rhys then leaped into the world of tech startups. As a non-technical founder, he navigated the tech seas, leading a team of contract developers in an agile environment. His startup quickly gained traction, attracting 5000 users and an advisory board member from Xero's founding team!

Rhys's tech journey didn't stop there. He plunged into the world of coding, spending nine months as a junior Salesforce developer with an Auckland company - Sleeq. Working with Salesforce's proprietary languages, along with JavaScript and Python, Rhys became an indispensable asset to his team.

Now, with approximately four years of experience as a self-employed contract mechanical engineer, Rhys stands at the intersection of engineering and tech. Armed with resilience, adaptability, and a relentless pursuit of growth, he's ready to conquer his next challenge.
